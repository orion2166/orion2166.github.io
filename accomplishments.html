ESCAPE FROM ALCATRAZ TRIATHLON June 12 2016

Escape from Alcatraz Triathlon features a 1.5-mile swim from Alcatraz Island to the shores of the St. Francis Yacht Club, a grueling 18-mile bike ride, and a demanding 8-mile run through the trails of the Golden Gate Recreational Area. Set against the natural beauty of San Francisco, this thrilling triathlon is a virtual postcard of the City by the Bay.

I had a great time being part of the Escape from Alcatraz Triathlon. I competed and completed the triathlon. It was, even more, fun to compete against the United States Rio Olympic Athletes. I didn't beat them but it was fun standing next to them and competing against them. This was one of the most physical events I attended and I hope to improve and do better next time.
